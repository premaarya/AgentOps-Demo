MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("MSA") is entered into as of February 1, 2026, by and between GlobalTech Solutions LLC, a New York limited liability company ("Provider"), and Meridian Enterprises Inc., a Texas corporation ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
1.1 Provider agrees to provide technology consulting, software development, and IT managed services as described in individual Statements of Work ("SOWs") executed under this MSA.
1.2 Each SOW shall be incorporated into and subject to the terms of this MSA.

2. TERM
2.1 This MSA shall be effective for an initial term of two (2) years from the date first written above.
2.2 The MSA shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
3.1 Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in each applicable SOW.
3.2 Payment terms are Net-45 from the date of invoice.
3.3 Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 All work product created by Provider specifically for Client under a SOW ("Client Work Product") shall be owned by Client upon full payment.
4.2 Provider retains all rights to its pre-existing intellectual property, tools, frameworks, and methodologies ("Provider IP").
4.3 Provider grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use Provider IP incorporated into Client Work Product.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
5.1 Each party agrees to hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict confidence.
5.2 Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this MSA for a period of three (3) years.

6. DATA PROTECTION
6.1 Provider shall process personal data only in accordance with Client's instructions and applicable data protection laws.
6.2 Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
6.3 Provider shall notify Client of any data breach within forty-eight (48) hours of discovery.

7. INDEMNIFICATION
7.1 Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other party from any third-party claims arising from the indemnifying party's negligence or willful misconduct.
7.2 Provider shall indemnify Client against claims of intellectual property infringement.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
8.1 Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.
8.2 Each party's total aggregate liability under this MSA shall not exceed three times (3x) the fees paid or payable in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

9. TERMINATION
9.1 Either party may terminate this MSA for convenience upon sixty (60) days written notice.
9.2 Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice.

10. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, or cyber attacks.

11. INSURANCE
Provider shall maintain: (a) Commercial General Liability insurance of at least $2,000,000; (b) Professional Liability insurance of at least $5,000,000; (c) Cyber Liability insurance of at least $3,000,000.

12. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
12.1 This MSA shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
12.2 Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association.

13. GENERAL PROVISIONS
13.1 This MSA may not be assigned by either party without prior written consent.
13.2 This MSA, together with all SOWs, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
13.3 Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this MSA as of the date first written above.
