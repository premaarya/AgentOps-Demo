AMENDMENT TO DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT

Amendment No. 1 ("Amendment") to the Data Processing Agreement dated September 1, 2025 ("Original Agreement"), between DataFlow Systems Corp., an Oregon corporation ("Processor"), and Pinnacle Health Group, a Massachusetts corporation ("Controller").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the parties entered into the Original Agreement governing the processing of personal data;
WHEREAS, the parties wish to amend certain provisions to comply with updated regulatory requirements and expand the scope of data processing activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. AMENDMENTS TO SECTION 2 - DATA PROCESSING SCOPE
1.1 Section 2.1 is amended to include processing of health-related personal data (PHI) in addition to general personal data (PII).
1.2 A new Section 2.4 is added: "Processor shall maintain HIPAA compliance for all PHI processing activities and provide annual compliance certifications."

2. AMENDMENTS TO SECTION 4 - SECURITY MEASURES
2.1 Section 4.1 is amended to require encryption at rest using AES-256 (previously AES-128).
2.2 Section 4.3 is amended to require quarterly penetration testing (previously annual).
2.3 A new Section 4.5 is added: "Processor shall implement zero-trust network architecture for all systems processing Controller's data by June 30, 2026."

3. AMENDMENTS TO SECTION 5 - DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
3.1 Section 5.1 is amended: breach notification timeline reduced from 72 hours to 24 hours for incidents involving PHI.
3.2 A new Section 5.4 is added: "Processor shall maintain a documented incident response plan, reviewed and tested semi-annually."

4. AMENDMENTS TO SECTION 7 - SUB-PROCESSORS
4.1 Section 7.2 is amended to require Controller's explicit written consent (not just notification) before engaging any new sub-processor for PHI processing.

5. AMENDMENTS TO SECTION 9 - FEES
5.1 Section 9.1 is amended: Monthly processing fee increased from $12,000 to $18,000 to reflect expanded scope and enhanced security requirements.
5.2 Fee increase effective April 1, 2026.

6. NO OTHER CHANGES
Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms and conditions of the Original Agreement remain in full force and effect.

7. EFFECTIVE DATE
This Amendment shall be effective as of April 1, 2026.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Amendment shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, consistent with the Original Agreement.
