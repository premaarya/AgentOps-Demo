DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

This Distribution Agreement is made effective June 1, 2026 between Vertex Foods Inc. ("Supplier") and Summit Distribution LLC ("Distributor").

1. APPOINTMENT
Supplier appoints Distributor as an authorized distributor for the Midwest territory.

2. ORDERS AND PAYMENT
Distributor shall pay all invoices within 30 days.

3. TERRITORY
Distributor has the right to distribute the products only within the agreed territory.
