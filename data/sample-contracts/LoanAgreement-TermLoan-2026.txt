LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement is made as of October 15, 2026 between Civic Lending Partners ("Lender") and Redwood Logistics LLC ("Borrower").

1. LOAN
Lender agrees to lend Borrower $1,200,000.

2. REPAYMENT
Borrower shall repay the loan in 24 monthly installments.

3. INTEREST
Interest rate shall be 9% per annum.
