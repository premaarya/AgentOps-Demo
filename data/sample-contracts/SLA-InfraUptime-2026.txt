SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") is entered into as of January 1, 2026, by and between InfraCloud Services Ltd., a UK limited company ("Provider"), and NorthStar Financial Corp., a Delaware corporation ("Client"), as a supplement to the Cloud Services Agreement dated November 15, 2025.

1. SERVICE OVERVIEW
This SLA covers the cloud infrastructure services provided by Provider to Client, including:
- Compute instances (Azure Virtual Machines)
- Managed databases (Azure SQL Database, Cosmos DB)
- Storage services (Blob Storage, File Shares)
- Network services (Virtual Network, Load Balancers, CDN)
- Security services (Azure Firewall, DDoS Protection)

2. UPTIME COMMITMENT
2.1 Provider guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime for all Tier 1 (production) services.
2.2 Provider guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for Tier 2 (staging/development) services.
2.3 Uptime is measured as: ((Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes) * 100.
2.4 Planned maintenance windows (Sundays 02:00-06:00 UTC) are excluded from uptime calculations.

3. SUPPORT RESPONSE TIMES
3.1 Severity 1 (Critical - Production Down): Response within 15 minutes, resolution target 4 hours.
3.2 Severity 2 (Major - Significant Impact): Response within 30 minutes, resolution target 8 hours.
3.3 Severity 3 (Minor - Limited Impact): Response within 2 hours, resolution target 24 hours.
3.4 Severity 4 (Low - General Questions): Response within 8 hours, resolution target 72 hours.

4. SERVICE CREDITS
4.1 Monthly uptime 99.00% to 99.95%: 10% credit of monthly service fees.
4.2 Monthly uptime 95.00% to 98.99%: 25% credit of monthly service fees.
4.3 Monthly uptime below 95.00%: 50% credit of monthly service fees.
4.4 Maximum service credits per month shall not exceed 50% of monthly service fees.
4.5 Credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident.

5. PERFORMANCE METRICS
5.1 API response time P95: less than 200ms for read operations, less than 500ms for write operations.
5.2 Database query response time P95: less than 100ms.
5.3 CDN cache hit ratio: greater than 90%.
5.4 Data durability: 99.999999999% (11 nines) for Blob Storage.

6. MONITORING AND REPORTING
6.1 Provider shall provide real-time monitoring dashboard accessible to Client 24/7.
6.2 Monthly SLA compliance reports delivered by the 5th business day of each month.
6.3 Quarterly business reviews including trending analysis and capacity planning.

7. DISASTER RECOVERY
7.1 Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour for Tier 1 services.
7.2 Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours for Tier 1 services.
7.3 Provider shall conduct disaster recovery tests semi-annually and share results with Client.

8. ESCALATION MATRIX
8.1 Level 1: Service Desk (initial contact)
8.2 Level 2: Technical Lead (30 minutes after Level 1)
8.3 Level 3: Service Delivery Manager (1 hour for Sev 1, 4 hours for Sev 2)
8.4 Level 4: VP of Operations (2 hours for Sev 1)

9. FEES
9.1 Monthly service fee: $45,000 (Forty-Five Thousand Dollars).
9.2 Payment terms: Net-30 from date of invoice.

10. TERM AND TERMINATION
10.1 This SLA is coterminous with the Cloud Services Agreement.
10.2 Client may terminate without penalty if Provider fails to meet the 99.95% uptime target for three (3) consecutive months.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This SLA shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
