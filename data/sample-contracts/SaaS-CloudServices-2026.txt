CLOUD SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Cloud Services Agreement is entered into as of September 1, 2026 between Nimbus Cloud Platforms ("Provider") and Meridian Financial Group ("Customer").

1. SERVICES
Provider shall make the software as a service platform available to Customer under a SaaS subscription agreement.

2. SERVICE LEVELS
Provider guarantees 99.95% uptime availability.

3. DATA PROTECTION
Customer data will be retained for no more than 3 years after termination unless law requires otherwise.

4. SECURITY
Provider shall maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards.
