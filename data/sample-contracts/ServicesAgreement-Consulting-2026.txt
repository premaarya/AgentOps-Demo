PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2026, by and between Northwind Advisory LLC ("Provider") and Contoso Retail Group ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider shall deliver consulting services, reporting modernization services, and operational support services.
